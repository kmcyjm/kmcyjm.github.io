facebook-ctf-2019

2019-06-04

In the past two days I spent some time on the Facebook CTF 2019.

This is the first CTF held by Facebook, and I would like to see what level it would be.

Right after I jump to its challenges page when the game opens, I realized that the challenges aren’t as trivial as the ones I’ve done in, e.g. picoCTF, or not even as easy to get started when I am doing Google CTFs.

It only has 2 - 3 challenge under each category, though more are released as the game goes on. It doesn’t have a beginner’s guide, and most of the challenges’s score are on the same level, which is indication saying “hey nerd, IT’S GONNA BE TOUGH!”
